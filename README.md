# Customer-Shopping-Behavior
Project Summary: Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

This project analyzes customer shopping behavior using Power BI, Python, and SQL to derive actionable business insights from a Kaggle sample dataset. The main objective is to understand customer purchasing patterns and identify key factors influencing sales performance.

Key Objectives

Analyze total revenue segmented by gender (male vs. female).

Compare sales performance based on shipping types (standard vs. express).

Identify the top 5 products by average purchase amount.

Examine the impact of discounts applied on overall sales.

Compare total revenue between subscribed and non-subscribed customers.

 Tools & Technologies

SQL – For data extraction, cleaning, and transformation.

Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn) – For data preprocessing and exploratory analysis.

Power BI – For building an interactive dashboard to visualize insights.

 Insights & Outcomes

Revenue distribution varies significantly by gender and subscription status.

Express shipping correlates with higher average order values.

Discounts have a noticeable effect on total sales and customer retention.

Top-performing products contribute disproportionately to total revenue.
 
Conclusion

The analysis provides a comprehensive view of customer behavior, helping businesses tailor marketing strategies, optimize discounts, and improve sales performance.
